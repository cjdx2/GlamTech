About Us

Welcome to GlamTech, where technology enhances beauty experiences. We are dedicated to improving salon operations and customer satisfaction through innovative, data-driven solutions. GlamTech utilizes AI to streamline appointment booking and salon management. Our system integrates advanced technologies like the ARIMA model and decision tree algorithms to personalize recommendations and forecast customer volumes accurately. Our systemized logbook with sales and commission analytics helps salon owners track performance and optimize resource allocation. By analyzing salon activities, we provide insights to recognize top-performing staff and ensure efficient operations. Experience the future of beauty care management with GlamTech.
